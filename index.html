It's an understatement to call these times "crazy". The world has changed in the last six months and not for the better. The panic is existent, spreading accross the world as the Coronavirus continues to manifest itself relentlessly in city after city. Unfortunately, this situation implied that travel is not a very clever idea. Instead of patching up last-minute plans for an epic summer of 2020 with the Olympics and a homecoming to the PHILIPPINES, we're on hold just like the rest of the world - simply hunkering down in our home and waiting this out.

The only thing to do is to follow the govenrment's orders to suppress the spread of the virus, keep our mind, body and soul healthy, be hopegul and look ahead to when life returns to normal. Once all this is over and precautions are lifted, we'll be free to head background-imageoutside, abroad, over oceans and borders to enjoy the staggering beauty and limitless freedom that only travel can deliver. While we're not able to travel now, we certainly can start dreaming and planning. Having said that, I have rounded up a handful of my favorite travel pieces to keep my wanderlust sparked during this dark times.
